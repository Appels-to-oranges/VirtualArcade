Copy your sound files here. Supported names (first match wins):

  shuffle.wav / shuffle.mp3
  card_put_down.wav, card put down.wav, or card_put_down.mp3
  BACKGROUND_CASINO_AMBIENCE.wav or BACKGROUND_CASINO_AMBIENCE.mp3
  winner.wav, winner together.wav, winner sound.wav, or winner.mp3
  your_turn.wav, your turn.wav, or your_turn.mp3
  chips_betting.wav or chips_betting.mp3 (plays on bet/raise/call)
  all_in.wav or all in.wav (plays on all-in)
  you_lose.wav or you lose.wav (plays when someone else wins)
  check.wav (plays on check)

To copy from Downloads: run copy-sounds.ps1 from the project root.
If files are missing, a fallback click sound is used.
